Greek Potatoes

Source

Recipe Tin Eats

Description

Potatoes cooked in a heavily flavoured lemon garlic broth.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 pounds yukon gold potatoes
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup
  • 3 finely grated garlic cloves
  • 1/2 tablespoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C/390°F.
  2. Cut potatoes: Peel potatoes and cut into wedges
  3. Coat potatoes: Place potatoes in a roasting pan with all the other ingredients. Toss well.
  4. Roast 45 minutes: Roast for 20 minutes. Turn potatoes, roast for a further 25 to 30 minutes until the liquid is mostly absorbed by potatoes/evaporated and you're left with mainly oil in the pan.
  5. Plate up: Transfer potatoes to serving platter. Drizzle over the reduced garlic pan juices (or toss potatoes in the pan). Serve, garnished with lemon wedges and oregano if desired.
